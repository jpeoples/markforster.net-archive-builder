What I have found is that the "want factor(WF)" for the items on the first 75% of my list is close to zero. Thus items on the first 75% of my list rarely beats the first selected item. (I view if both WF's are zero, then no new item is selected.)

The action only starts when I get to my last section of the list. This is where the WF shoots up and I can really measure items against each other as the WF rises above zero.

This suggests you're misreading the question. It's not "Want" but "Want Before". I presume you really don't want the first item, so usually there will be something in that first 75% that you would rather do BEFORE you get to that first one. The idea is to ramp up your work in levels of increasing difficulty until you get to the hard one at the front of the list when you are primed to tackle it.
