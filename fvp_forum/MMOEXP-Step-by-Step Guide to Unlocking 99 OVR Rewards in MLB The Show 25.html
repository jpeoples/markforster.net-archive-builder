What is the Postseason Past Program?




The Postseason Past Program in MLB The Show 25 celebrates historical postseason heroes and memorable performances. Each program typically features a selection of past MLB stars, complete with cards that replicate their real-life stats and accomplishments. This is your chance to add Hall of Famers and legendary players to your roster-many of whom are equipped with unique abilities, incredible hitting or pitching ratings, and the potential to drastically improve your team's lineup.




Unlike live events or weekly programs, the Postseason Past Program focuses on completing specific objectives and collections. It challenges players to earn points, complete missions, or participate in matchups that reflect the players' historical performances. The ultimate reward? Some of the highest-rated cards in the game, including rare 99 OVR cards.




How to Get Started




To begin, navigate to the Postseason Past Program menu in MLB The Show 25's Diamond Dynasty. Here, you'll see a series of missions or challenges that are tied to specific past MLB seasons or players. These may include:




Mission Objectives: Complete tasks such as hitting home runs, striking out a certain number of batters, or stealing bases within a limited number of games.




Matchups: Play against CPU or online opponents in scenarios that replicate historical playoff moments. Success in these matchups earns points or program tokens.




Collections: Some cards are only available after completing sets of historic players. Collecting these cards unlocks bonus rewards and higher-tier items, including elite equipment and packs.




Before jumping in, it's important to prioritize the objectives that award the most program points or tokens. Some missions are easier to complete and offer incremental progress, while others are more difficult but provide significant rewards. Understanding the scoring system will help you maximize your efficiency.




Earning 99 OVR Cards




The ultimate draw of the Postseason Past Program is the 99 OVR cards. These cards are extremely rare and powerful, often representing legendary hitters, pitchers, or two-way players from baseball history. Here's how to earn them:




Complete Milestone Objectives

Each Postseason Past Program typically has milestone objectives that track your progress across multiple missions. Reaching key milestones unlocks elite cards, and some of these milestones are directly tied to earning a 99 OVR player. Focus on completing all milestones for maximum rewards.




Finish Collections

Many 99 OVR cards are gated behind completing collections of other historic players. For instance, completing a set of past MVPs or World Series heroes might reward you with a top-tier card. Make sure to check the required cards for each collection and plan your gameplay accordingly.




Participate in Live Events

Some programs include live-event challenges, where completing specific tasks in real-time matches contributes to program points. Consistently participating in these events can accelerate your path to 99 OVR rewards.




Use Program Tokens Efficiently

As you complete missions and matchups, you'll earn program tokens. These tokens can be redeemed for packs or specific player cards within the Postseason Past Program. Prioritize using tokens on rewards that contribute toward 99 OVR cards rather than on smaller, incremental prizes.

Tips to Maximize Your Progress

Leverage Player Abilities: Use historic players in your lineup who have abilities that align with the program's objectives. For example, if a mission requires multiple stolen bases, insert players with high speed and base-stealing traits.




Plan for Collections: Review the program's collection requirements in advance. Acquiring certain cards early can make it easier to complete larger sets and unlock milestone rewards.




Consistency is Key: Postseason Past Program progress is cumulative. Play daily, complete missions, and participate in matchups regularly to steadily advance toward elite rewards.
