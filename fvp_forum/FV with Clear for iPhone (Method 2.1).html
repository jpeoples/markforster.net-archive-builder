Let's get started...


SET UP YOUR LIST:

1. Create a new list in Clear.

2. Create a task with something like the following in it. Completely fill the task box so it fills one line only. This is your divider:

⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷⊷
__________________

.....................................

------------------------------

••••••••••••••••••••••••••••••

******************************

Swipe this divider to the right to dim it.

3. Create another task with any of these questions inspired by Lillian and Seraphim from this post http://www.markforster.net/fv-forum/post/1803934 and tap the spacebar a few times to place it in the center of the task box:

DO WHAT BEFORE X?
BUT FIRST, I'LL DO...
BEFORE X I'LL?
BEFORE X?

I prefer BEFORE X?.

Any of these will serve as a reminder of the Final Version question "What do I want to do before I do x?" while maintaining the emphasis on "Before" and are easily repeatable while scanning down your list.


HOW TO USE THE CLEAR METHOD:

1. Add your tasks by tapping in the black area at the bottom of your list.

Your list will look similar to this:

http://dl.dropbox.com/u/345611/Photos/Final_Version/IMG_9229.PNG

2. Build a chain by swiping the first task at the top of your list to the right. Your list will now look similar to this:

http://dl.dropbox.com/u/345611/Photos/Final_Version/IMG_9230.PNG

3. Continue scanning down your list and when you come to a task you want to do before your previous selection, swipe it to the right.

In the example list below I've chosen the last task on my list as the one I want to do before my previous selection. I swipe it to the right and it looks like this:

http://dl.dropbox.com/u/345611/Photos/Final_Version/IMG_9231.PNG

4. When there are no further tasks you wish to do before your previously selected task, touch the bottom of the screen where the iPhone trim meets the touchscreen, just above the iPhone home button to shoot to the bottom of your list.

You will now see a dimmed list of your preselected tasks and below that the divider you created earlier.

5. Beginning at the bottom of your dimmed list, swipe your dimmed divider to the right. Do the same for each bottommost dimmed task until all tasks are illuminated.

Your list will now look similar to this:

http://dl.dropbox.com/u/345611/Photos/Final_Version/IMG_9232.PNG

6. Swipe a task left to delete it when complete, or swipe it right to send it to the end of your list if recurring or incomplete.

To add new future tasks with an active chain, simply tap the black area at the bottom of your list, create your new task, and swipe it right to send it to the end of your list.

To add urgent tasks to your current chain, tap in the black area at the bottom of your list and create your new task. It will now be the first task in your chain.

7. When you have completed your chain and all its tasks are either dimmed or deleted, swipe the divider to the right to dim it as well.

8. Now send all your dimmed tasks to the end of your list. Swipe the bottommost dimmed task to the right and do the same for each bottommost dimmed task until all tasks are illuminated, but leave the divider dimmed.

You are now ready to add new tasks or create another chain.


That's it!

Here's a terser summary of the steps:

0. Check that all tasks are brightened above dimmed divider at bottom.
1. Swipe-right to dim root task at top of list (and send it to bottom).
2. Swipe-right to dim one or more tasks going down the list, according to Question.
3. Swipe-right to brighten divider line (and move it above selected chain).
4. Swipe-right from bottom of chain going up. Everything is bright again.
5. Do chain bottom-up; swipe-left if finished/nonrecurring; swipe-right otherwise.
6. Swipe-right to dim divider.
7. Swipe-right from bottom up to brighten any unfinished/recurring tasks.

It makes sense, but is a little tricky to keep tasks in the right order. Remember to start the chain by brightening the divider, and end the chain by dimming the divider. Everything else is top-down preselection and bottom up working/swiping, very consistent with the paper-based procedure.

Terserer:

1. Right-swipe first task at top.
2. Scroll down preselecting with right-swipe.
3. Right-swipe each dim task at bottom until all are lit.
4. Work chain bottom up. Left-swipe to delete. Right-swipe to send to end of list.
5. When only the divider remains lit, right-swipe it.
6. Repeat step 3, but leave the divider dim.
