I was inspired by a recent post on incremental timeboxing and I've been using it over the past month with great effect. To help myself along, I created a PC application that takes an ordinary todo list and converts it into an incremental timebox list complete with progress bars and tones to let me know when to go to the next task. It's free and I'd like to share it- here's the link: http://ericjorgensen.com/TimeBoxer.html

Please try it out and let me know how you like it, or better yet, let me know how I can improve it.
