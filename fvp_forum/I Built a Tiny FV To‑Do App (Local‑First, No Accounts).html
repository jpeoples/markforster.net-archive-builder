I created a small app specifically for Mark Forster’s systems. Most to‑do tools are built around GTD and never quite fit FV for me, so I made Evergreen: a simple, local‑first list with a Focus view. You can dot tasks to pre‑select a chain and then work that chain in reverse in Focus. No accounts, no sync, just your list in the browser. I’d love your thoughts and suggestions: https://evergreen-todo.vercel.app/ (Feedback → Settings). Not affiliated, just a fan who wanted a digital way to run the list.
