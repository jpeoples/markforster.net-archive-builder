I really like FV. There are some good things I like but some other not. I like the AF look. I like the dots.

When I used AF I liked the dissmiss and it simplicity. With FV I am like I was with AF and it is simple to work on my stuff.

On another hand, when I used SF I liked the reporting of urgent and unfinished on the right column, but I did not like the right column itself.

Now I try to mixt the systems the find the best of them.
1. My idea is to keep FV including in my spiral notebook everything as the happen. Including notes. One after the other and keep the right column for decision about some items
2. My idea is also to put a star in front of all urgent task so they will be considered as dot and acted first
3. My last idea is to high line all task which are unfinished or to follow and decide what to do about them asap.
4. My last idea is not to pass to the following page until I did not work on urgencies or unfinished tasks
5. I keep the following list of my project on word pages
6. And the most important and complicated project on word pages.
7. OF will be reserved for future items
