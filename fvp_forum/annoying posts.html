matthewS:

There isn't a moderation option on the forums. The choices available are:

1) No control at all. Attracts a large number of spam posts, very difficult to keep on top of them.

2) Captcha (the present setting). Cuts the number of spam posts down to zero most days, with occasional bursts of activity, which are relatively easy to clear. Unfortunately the last burst of spamming activity, a particularly large one, took place in a period when I didn't look at the forums for several days.

3) Members only posting. Frankly a pain in the neck both for posters and for me - who has to sort out all the problems with it. There are only a limited number of membership slots available on my current subscription. To increase them, would cost me more.

My conclusion is therefore: thanks for the offer of help, but in fact the present arrangement is about as good as it gets.
