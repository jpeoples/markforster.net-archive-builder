What happens in the FV system if the first task of the list can't be worked on because there's another related task in the list that needs to be done before?

Reply by Mark Forster

2013-10-8 9:08
Since the related task is a condition of doing the first task, you can subsume the related task into the first task.

I take it as a general principle not related to FV, when such happens make a list of these tasks and in the FV list simply refer to that list once. Highlight the first task todo in that reference, and also note the bigger picture of why you are doing it.
