Like some others, I'm also intrigued by the Kanban approach.. After dabbling with it last year in an online version, I think I still prefer the manual, whiteboard,sheet of paper, back of the door approach.

Right now I'm fighting to complete key tasks before I leave Damascus on Thursday for an extended break (all right, 'right now' I'm typing this post!).

I've put each task on a small Post-it slip (50mm x 35mm), stuck the waiting ones down the left hand side (plastic frame) of my notebook screen, the current one at the top, the finished ones on the right (only one so far).

Very visual, seems to be helping, I think I'll try it again when I need to focus on my notebook screen and limit distractions.
