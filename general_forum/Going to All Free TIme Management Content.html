I basically decided the time management stuff was really a separate topic and now quite simply give it away because there is so much free time management stuff and low cost books, that it is hard to make money in the niche unless you really ramp up and do a full blown book. I personally embrace simplicity and could never write a full book on the topic as I don't think complex systems are needed. The site does generate traffic and helps sell DOIT, which I think is a more unique product and plays a more important role in my life and hopefully the lives of others.

DOIT is still charged for at $12.99, but I added a money back guaranty. If you do the program and it does get you closer to your goals I refund your money. I only ask people send me the last page. I don't mind refunding if people actually do the program. I don't want to refund for people who "think" it won't work but did not attempt it. It is the exact program I used to accomplish several important things in my life and was heavily influenced by the Science of Getting Rich. The same book the Secret crowd discovered. However, I focused on the action chapters while they focused on the manifesting and attraction parts. As I have written about, the public is easier sold attraction versus action, it seems more mystical and is less work.

One thing I have considered is a beta test like what Mark did where I would ask people to use the program and post their experiences. However, I am concerned that the program would be "tweaked" and the test would get sidetracked. But, would love to hear peoples thoughts about the idea of participating.
