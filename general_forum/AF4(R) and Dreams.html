I'm playing with incorporating Dreams into AF4 this way: I regard the largest "Unfinished" item as my future vision. I list various activities from "Dreams" and elsewhere as an AF-style list on the "unfinished" page. Then when working on my future "stands out" from the AF1 list I use the AF(1) process to go over the various activities in "unfinished" until the right one "stands out". I feel this is helping me achieve an (implicit) goal to balance living in the moment with setting aside time for thinking about my future. A pleasing outcome.
