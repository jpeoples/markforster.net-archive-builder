What is the holiday-mood and how can you recreate it?

During holidays people become very focused on

- caring for themselves, caring about feeling good and relaxing

- engaging in activities that bring them joy

- offloading cares and worries

- appreciation for good things and flow (not resisting living)

So now and again make this a project, with checklist. Now that's what I call success.
