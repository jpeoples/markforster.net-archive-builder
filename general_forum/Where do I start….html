Hello New guy,

you can divide Mark's systems in three broad categories:
- Long List systems (such as AF1, FVP, Simple Scanning etc)
- No List systems (5/2, No List FVP ect)
- more or less day-based systems (DIT, DWM, Dreams)

Most principles voiced in our discussions here relate to either one of these groups or all of them at the same time.

Where should you start? Well, what's your goal exactly?

Conversely, what challenges are you facing in your work and life at the moment?
