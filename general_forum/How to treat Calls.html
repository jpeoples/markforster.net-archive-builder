Since i use Autofocus there is a major problêm about Calls. I think i found a solution that i share with you.

Calls are my major activities. In my job most of things urgent or important are better done by call. Here is how i proceed.

Separate tasks and calls : I have a special page for call and tasks are all together on the next page.

ex. Call Mr smith 01 02 03 04 05 / for making an appointement next monday about project BOOM)

- If the call is done then cross the whole line

- If the call need a following put a x at the begining and an indicator at the end
ex. X Call Mr smith 01 02 03 04 05 / for making an appointement next monday about project / M$ 05/13 (ie i let a message today)

- the x means ok i done that but i need to follow it

- Act in Af as usual (2 readings) skipping all x to make your others calls

The result is a good focus on call and a real efficiency in the treatement of my important and urgent items.
