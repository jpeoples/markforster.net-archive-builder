2009-01-6 22:21
Jason:

No, you're not right there. If there's just one task left on page 1 and you come back to page 1 and don't do that task, it is dismissed (by being highlighted) and the page is closed. The dismissed task should not be re-entered (if at all) without your examining whether it should be broken down or re-phrased in some way.

Re your second question: bear in mind that you only HAVE to take action on one task per page and that task need only be worked on for a short while. That means that you could progress from page 2 to page 7 in a matter of minutes.
