Actually, as long as you have worked on at least one item on your current page, you can move on to the next page. So you don't have to complete all of the items on page 5 before moving on to page 6. Even if you've done just one thing on page 5, you can move on to page 6. And you don't dismiss any of the items on page 5 because you worked on at least one of them.

You only dismiss a page if you come to it and *none* of the items stand out, and you don't do anything on any of the items left.

Tommy:

What you are missing is the rather important word "first" in your final quote from the instructions.

You only dismiss items if nothing stands out for you on your FIRST pass through a pass when you visit it, i.e. if you don't do at least one item on that page that visit.
