I lauch a new thread of some tips we use about AF- They concern all AF. I hope every body will participate ti share ideas and improvements.

I use AFR (it AF4R less 4). It's an hybrid system of AF and AF4R.
To explain imagine just a AF1 list with page separated like in AF4R...
Pages are treated one by one and dissmissed like on AF1.

Pages are separated like this. ASAP (OLD and NEW)/ UNFINISHED/ RECURRING TASKS/ TIKLER by quarter/ DO EVENTUALLY/ PROJECT LIST/

The system works perfectly well.

This tips concern is adaptable to every AF and is about my 2011 objectives. I will try to do them until the end of the year.

It consist of simply grouping all relative tasks together by concerned pages.

Ex in my ASAP page I took from AF all tasks concerning my organization and my acounts to treat them one by one. Crossed them at the to and reported at the end of the page.

example :
Strategy 2011
- Decide what to do and how for 2011
-

Spreadsheet (tool)
-Update my building list for 2011
- Update my client's list for 2011
- Update building's seller for 2011
- Update real estate company i want to work with for 2011
- Eliminate every one on my data base i don't work to work with

Account (tool)
- Make a spread sheet about my business previsional
- Update my spread sheet about expenses previsional s

This help me to focus on some kinds of projects at a very short term.
