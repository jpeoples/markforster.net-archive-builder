My $.02 on the Dojo Guide - bear in mind I haven't bought it and I won't be either. The two guys behind it are Charley Gilkey from www.productiveflourishing.com and Jonathan Mead from www.illuminatedmind.net.

Charlie Gilkey is an extremely sharp young guy and his site is really great. It used to be easier to navigate, but if you surf it by categories you can get the gist of his style. He has a background in philosophy (I think he has his doctorate in philosophy), and it shows in his writing.

Jonathan is a ~24 y.o. rebel type of guy. His writing is very inspiring I would imagine to some of that age group who has the wanting to break free of the corporate grind mentality. I'm not sure how his methods - whatever they are - would work for someone who doesn't have a similar mindset.

My guess is that the guide would be along the lines of inspirational (ie. do what you love talk) rather than time management prescriptive. I could be wrong though.

They have a money back guarantee I think though and Charlie is a super guy and would definitely honor it. I wouldn't hesitate to get the materials in the knowledge that you could return them if it wasn't up your alley.
