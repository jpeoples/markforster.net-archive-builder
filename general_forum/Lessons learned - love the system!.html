Hi Mark:

Just wanted to say that I love the Autofocus system! I realized that I've used it before but somehow drifted away into more complicated schemes.

One thing I've learned is the importance of not rewriting your lists. I experimented with doing a "weekly review" and rewriting everything so that it was nice and pretty. But after watching your video I realized how I was defeating the whole purpose and have now returned to the fold.

I used pink highlighter to cross out items I've worked on or finished and green highlighter to mark items that I've dismissed or reevaluated. I think the mass of color helps because it serves as a visual representation of the momentum I'm feeling and makes the items I'm avoiding really stand out. The fact that I can't "complete" the page serves as inspiration to either work on the remaining items or rethink them.

Thanks for the great idea. It's really helped me, more than GTD ever did.

Best,
Jennifer
