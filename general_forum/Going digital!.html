Sarah,

Do a search here on Toodledo for a complete description of how I did it before I scrapped it all. I implemented DWM in that software, then just as easily converted it to my simple priority/schedule system. It is a web based application with an iPhone app to access it. I'm sure it will work on the iPad. Ideal because you can do power typing on your computer and just check things off on your portable device. Basically you can set the default entry date for new items to one month hence and it keeps things sorted as entered. It has unlimited tagging, folders, goals, priority ... you name it. Give it a quick look on the net and see what you think.
