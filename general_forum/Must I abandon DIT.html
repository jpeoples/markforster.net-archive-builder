Steve, all of mark's books are excellent and DIT was far and away the best system I had found prior to AF. (You will see the difference between the two when you get your copy of the book).

For me AF is so much more flexible and does away with feelings of guilt if you don't finish you day's tasks under DIT. Whilst I have a similar volume of "stuff" coming in each day, other unalterable commitments meant that I only had two full days each week where I could action things, plus a few snatched hours. The result was that on most days I could not complete my list and became discouraged. That was not a fault of the system but a problem with my time structure - AF eliminates that difficulty for me.
