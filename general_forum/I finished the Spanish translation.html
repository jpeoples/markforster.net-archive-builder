Hello Walter!

Concerning the wiki: For starters, you could just go to http://wiki.autofocus.cc/Spanish_Translation and click on "edit this page". Then Copy your text there and save it.

Then we can work on the formatting. Some examples for wiki formatting are:

Headings:

== This is a heading on level two ==

=== This is a heading on level three ===

Lists:

* unordered
* bullet
* list

# ordered
# numbered
# list

If you're interested, you can look at the other translation pages to see how they are made. Alternatively you can simply send your document to andreas at autofocus.cc and I'll add it to the wiki for you.
