New idea to me: besides including todos, chores, and projects, I've taken to writing down helpful words in my list:
- quotes
- goals/dreams
- principles
- bible verses
- things to be mindful of
- helpful thoughts (like from CL, Do what needs doing and accept your feelings)

These aren't actionable, but they change my mindset for whatever actionable work follows.
I allow myself to delete or reenter these non-tasks at will. Be careful not to dismiss your dreams.
