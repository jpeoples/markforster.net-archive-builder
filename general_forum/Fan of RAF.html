I just wanted to thank Mark for continuing to tinker. I tried diligently to get AF1 to work for me, but it never quite did. I hardly got interested in AF2. But RAF is working great for me. In the previous iterations, I avoided my notebook for fear that by opening it I was committing to either do or dismiss something. Now, with RAF, if nothing stands out, I can just shut the book and go play. Likewise, "change to forward mode" may not stand out for days on end. With RAF, I find it much easier to open the notebook and get something done, instead of avoiding my notebook altogether. Thanks again.
