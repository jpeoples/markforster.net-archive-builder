Mark-

I watched the video and it was eye-opening to see how you use Autofocus.

I have one suggestion that would make finding the first active page in a notebook full of pages easier:
Simply CLIP or TEAR off the CORNER of the completed page. If only one side of a two-sided page is complete, FOLD the corner (then clip it when both sides are complete).

That way, you can easily see and feel where you are.
