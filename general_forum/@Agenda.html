Borisoff,

That is outside of AF proper, as is a Tickler, Calendar, Contacts, etc. AF4 is for To Do items ... and it also functions as a universal capture point, if you use it that way (i.e. put everything into it first.)

I would suggest that you either expand how much you write in the AF entry for the meeting; put it on your calendar; keep a separate agenda list, etc. One thing you can do is keep your AF book from front to back and then have special lists from back to front ... i.e. put your ticklers and agendas and special projects lists from back to front. That works for me.
