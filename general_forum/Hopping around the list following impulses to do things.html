Jacqueline:

Can I be "psychic" again and say that I don't think either dumping your entire Flylady control list into AF or restricting the areas of interest that you put in AF is the right way to proceed.

The recommended way is to let your list grow organically. That is to say, start it with a few items of immediate concern and just add other tasks as you think of them or as they come up. Resist any tendency to dump things en masse into into AF, but also resist any tendency to censor what you put on the list.

That way you will have a list which is alive and relevant to what your actual current concerns are.
