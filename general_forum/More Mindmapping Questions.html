Like Will mentioned for Freemind, Freeplane (a fork of Freemind) has tons of export options.
Export > Branch as HTML (Ctrl-H)
Export > As PNG...

It's not "Copy" and it creates a separate file, but it should get you a pretty map in Word.

Also, like Freemind, Freeplane will copy/paste a nested bulleted list into Word.
