My future reality is comprehensive and I feel that it's too long. But whenever I try to shorten it, I end up losing the feelings attached to the pieces I have shortened. - scholarship

My understandings is that we zoom in and out of various parts of our future reality, using the goal achievement method. So I'm not sure if I've put stuff into my future reality vision that belongs better in goals.

Is anyone else encountering this? Are there problems with having too lengthy a future reality?

An update, for what it's worth:

I am back to every-morning revisions of my Future Vision and Present Reality. I open the text file containing them, and then I open a new blank window right alongside. Looking at the "real" file, I manually retype it into the blank window. After each paragraph, I stop to review and revise. When finished, I "Select All" in the new window and copy/paste into the "real" file.

This process feels comfortable enough now, though I sometimes have to use "just get the folder out" to make it happen instead of rushing off to do the day's work. I've been very happy with my vision's content, and just yesterday it got a big overhaul which has me beaming!

Earlier, in some other thread, I reported having a 2500-word vision. Umm, that was actually 2500 characters. Characters!! I had misread a cryptic display in my editor. My current version is at 140 words (Words!) and looks to be about 40% of its former size.
