Since I am back to timeboxing, I've discovered three nice countdown timers that are available as web pages.

1. Google! Just enter "timer" into http://www.google.com/ and you get a basic countdown timer.
It comes with a stopwatch in another tab.

2. http://www.timeanddate.com/timer/ - the most flexible. Allows multiple timers on one page with customizable names and alert sounds. You can start them all at once or separately by hand, or each can start automatically after the prior one stops.

3. http://e.ggtimer.com/ - very simple, has interesting modes such as Pomodoro, URL-based so you can bookmark a specific setup. Pops up an alert dialog on expiration, which pushes its window to the front of your screen.
