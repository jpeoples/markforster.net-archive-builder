Here's how I'm doing it -
Get a notebook with dated pages or date the pages yourself
Write new tasks on March 5 (one month from day)
When I work on one of the tasks, I cross it off March 5.
If it's not finished when I'm done working on it (little&often), I write it on Feb 12 (one week from day)
Tomorrow, new tasks go on March 6, worked on but not finished tasks go on Feb 13. I have tasks to pick from on 4 pages - Feb 12, 13 and March 5, 6.
On Feb 12 (probably at the end of the day, I haven't decided) whatever's not crossed-off is dismissed.

You only work on pages dated between today and "today + 30" Pages before today are dismissed and pages greater than "today +30" are blank.

Or you can do the same set up electronically.

Not an expert, but I think you would re-enter the task again, seven days on from the day you worked on it, eg if worked on it on Feb 5, re-enter Feb 12, then work again on Feb 7, re-enter on Feb 14, and so on.

Each time you work on a task, you have seven more days to come back to it before it will expire.
