I have used AF4 and DWM system but this system I explain here works well to me.

Some years ago I read about one Time management system that was based on some psychology study. Some fortune 500 CEO's use this system paper based
I have lost t he pdf file :(

I will try to explain it let see if you understand.

Each task you do need to be done between 2 and 5 times unit.
Time unit is hour , day , week , year. Other time unit can be use but I use those.

Example :

Hour.
You call a customer and he says call me back later today. Then you should call him back between 2 to 5 hours from now.
1 hours is to quick and 6 hours is to faraway.

Day.
You send an email to a friend / contact. You can/should remind him between 2 to 5 days from now.

Weeks.
You order some books on one e-shop. The product should arrive between 2 to 5 weeks.

Today I use one Google spreadsheet that I can read / update in my mobile phone. Very basic spreadsheet but works for me.
