Congratulations on the new position, getting the new position is the realization of a desired outcome. A new one will be excelling in the new position. 2010-04-14 17:04
I don't believe in percentage complete myself. I do believe in assigning points for full completion though. So Mum's present is worth 40 points, and Dad's is 10 points or whatever. The distinction is twofold:

1. You only get points for finishing something. So those subgoals need to be concrete, and you can't claim "room is half-clean", as a nebulous half-done doesn't count for much.

2. It doesn't necessarily add up to 100. You can do smaller projects for smaller points, and combine several [complete several] for bigger points.
