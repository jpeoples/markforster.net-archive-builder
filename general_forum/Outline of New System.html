Hi Avrum,

let me give your questions a shot:

I interpret the new System as beeing an Autofocus list with built in dismissal dates. Every task remains active for at least one month. Once you start working on something, you only get a week to continue. All pages later than today are active, all pages earlier are dismissed. The way I understand it is that the calendar is not serving a daily planning purpose but is only serving as an automatic dismissal or deletion mechanism.

Put differently: if you have taken on an obligation and haven't started working on it within a month, then there is possibly something wrong with it. This means that you need to analyze what is wrong.

The proposed system is clearly a task management system and not a time management system, despite being run inside a calendar.

Oh, and to your #3, once a task is finished, you take it from the list.

Hope this helps,

Klaus
