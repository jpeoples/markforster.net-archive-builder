Andreas,

Great job on that. I'm sure it will prove helpful to those of us (like myself) who appreciate visual confirmation that were working through things the right way.

I know there is a limit to the level of detail and complexity one may want from a flowchart, but here are couple of things to consider:

1) Your purple box "Go to the next unactioned task before the current one" might benefit from this addition ", if there is one, otherwise return to the end of list and continue to work in Reverse Mode". I'd leave it to you how the flow arrows would map out of that.

2) Your blue diamond "Was it your first pass through the current page" leads directly to "Re-add change to FM" (and then to Reverse Mode). However, you might benefit from an additional diamond fork between the two -- a diamond with the question "Is this the LAST page?" A "yes" would take you to the first page and continuing on in FM, and a "no" would take you to reverse mode. Does that make sense?

Folks! Can anyone verify for me my point in #2 above that if you are in forward mode, and get to your last page, and you don't take any action on that page that you should go to first page and continue in forward mode?

Thank you!
--Joseph
