Hi all

I was searching the forum for something and came across this old (pre AF2) posting about the advantages/disadvantages of having more than one list:

http://www.markforster.net/forum/post/764772#post764839

When I was working with AF1 I found I needed different lists for different areas of my life (one for home, one for my painting, etc.) and yet I've found since changing to AF2 I have one main list and only make other lists for very specific things like shopping. I started a new list from scratch for AF2 as I'd actually stopped using AF1 some time before Mark announced AF2. Yet now I'm finding my one list, even when used quite sloppily, is keeping me on top of tasks very well. Also the new dismissal system is working much better at sorting the wheat from the chaff!! By allowing myself a fair bit of flexibility with how I use my list I'm finding it much, much easier to keep going with AF2 whereas with AF1 by now I would have stopped using it altogether.

Has anyone else found any of these types of changes in how they use AF1 and AF2?
