I think what he says about intuition is basically what I have been saying for ages (and for which I claim no originality):

Intuition isn't some divine revelation from above, but the voice of your accumulated knowledge and experience. I often give the example of a fire chief giving instructions during a fire, or a pilot dealing with an emergency situation. Both situations require split-second reactions which they do intutively. But without their knowledge and experience, they wouldn't have the intuition.

Also worth reading are his thoughts on time management, which he gives a link to in his article. http://terrytao.wordpress.com/2008/08/07/on-time-management/
