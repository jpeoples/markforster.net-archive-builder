Hi,

I test create AF4 list with my email/smartphone client.
I use virtual.nu - The have a new release - Thats why I test this again
Free and with not ads . http://www.virtual.nu/ ( right now )

I will explain how I do.

Scheduler at virtual.nu have as default reminder on 3 days.

sched@virtual.nu : To schedule 3 days from now.
sched+tomorrow@virtual.nu : To schedule tomorrow same time
sched+1000-tomorrow@virtual.nu : To schedulte tomorrow at 1000

Then I use a label in gmail to get all reminders from virtual.nu in a list that I work on like I do on paper.
If I need to do more work on it I resend same email to sched@virtual.nu.


If I have an email I need to work on later or need later forward the email so I get the email back when I need it.

Like I need my electronic ticket for my flight on thursday I send

sched+0600-thu@virtual.nu

Maybe someone use a system like that ?

Any tips or tricks from you ?
