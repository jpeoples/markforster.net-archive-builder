James,
I actually used Keynote (Apple's presentation software), with each "step" being one slide. The animation part was easy because I could just duplicate the last slide and then move the arrow object and quickly format the text as necessary. And Keynote can export to PDF and, more importantly for me for this project, Quicktime.

(I tried it initially with Numbers (the Apple spreadsheet) but it wasn't as intuitive to animate the step by step process there, nor could I have exported it as a movie.)
