Mark,
Learning how to play musical instruments is far easier than you might think. When I needed to learn how to play various instruments simultaneously (mostly for music composition), I'd set the timer to 30minutes for each one daily. If I wasn't connecting well with one, I'd give it a bit more effort. The good news is that learning the rudiments of playing an instrument well enough to either enjoy it or how to properly write music for it, is surprisingly fast. It gets difficult when you start aiming for expertise.........but, I suppose that's true for most things....and people foolishly believe that having talent gives a person an edge. It helps us to breeze through the novice and intermediate levels of proficiency but.........when you start to desire reaching and maintaining expert levels, that's where the grinding begins! LOL! (p.s....If it's a true passion, it's totally worth it.)
