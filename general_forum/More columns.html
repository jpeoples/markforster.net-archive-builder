It occurs to me that SF3 can be extended to have more than two columns. For example, if you have two jobs, you could put tasks for one job in column 1, and tasks for the other in column 2. Column 3 would then have the unfinished / urgent items in it. You then have to advance at least one task in column1 and one task in column 2, as well as clearing column 3, when processing any page.

I'm dabbling with using column 1 for the next steps taken from top-down, goal-directed, project-managed life planning , and column 2 for ad-hoc stuff that comes in and isn't part of those plans.
