For a digital solution I would also recommend SimpleNote, which is a note-taking app for the iPhone that improves upon the built-in notes app and syncs with a website. SimpleNote can also be used in conjunction with Nottingham, which is a clone/redo of the incredible 'Notational Velocity', a note-taking app for the mac.

I don't use either for my primary AF4 list, but I use it for shopping lists, a quick inbox, and other assorted notes/tasks (either using the AF system, or just plain note-taking).
