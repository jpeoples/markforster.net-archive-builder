Hello everyone,

one of Mark's goals is for Revised Autofocus to beat the number of translations of the original Autofocus instructions. To help with this, I propose that everyone who is willing but has no way to publish a translation himself use the autofocus wiki at http://wiki.autofocus.cc

Currently there already are three translations there:

http://wiki.autofocus.cc/German_Translation by me
http://wiki.autofocus.cc/Italian_Translation by Romano
http://wiki.autofocus.cc/Japanese_Translation by jk

With the off-site Estonian and Simplified Chinese translations, that makes 5. OAF had 16, so we still have a way to go.

If you'd like to help, please feel free to create any translation you want in the wiki. Thank you very much.
