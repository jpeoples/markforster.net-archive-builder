The first time I dismissed an item in AF I added a task to the end of the list of 'review dismissed items' then when this stands out I will review anything that's been dismissed to date.

So far on reviewing dismissed items I have permanently removed from the AF list as a subsequent set of actions has superseded it, another item has been re-added to the list as it's now appropriate to do this actionThe remaining dismissed items are still being removed as the review task stands out for me.

Hope this makes sense and helps
