Avrum:

It doesn't really work like that. There's a world of difference between deciding on impulse to go to an unproductive website (for example), and writing down a task to visit that website. Writing it down brings different parts of the brain into use.

Also, if you record the dates that you do items, you can see exactly what you are doing with your time, which is in itself a big incentive to do better!
