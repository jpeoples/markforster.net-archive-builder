Just an observation (not a particularly big concern):

As I'm in backward mode (ie AF2), I often find myself going past a recent task that 'almost' stands out (or "ready to be done") but which I feel may be superseded by something earlier on the list. After reviewing another couple of pages, I realize that this first task is probably the best one to be doing right now and I end up going back to it. This feels a little like I'm in AF1/closed list mode, i.e., where I would review a set of tasks at least once before choosing one. At worst, it feels a little like I'm just using my AF list as a traditional To Do list.

Maybe this is simply how AF2 works in practice. Do other people tend to operate in the same way, ie scanning up the list before settling on a task that "almost" stands out?

Andy
