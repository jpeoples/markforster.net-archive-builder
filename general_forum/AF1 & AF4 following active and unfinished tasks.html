It's the end of the week. Since tuesday I tried something very simple.

I am so please because the system i use now is so simple and so easy that i wonder why i never thought about it before...

This is what i did.
Keep my binder for my projects and my goals. Treat it as GTD project manner.

Tasks
Put AF1 on a one page per day agenda a cheap travers A5 (French) 2,50 € !
Divide it page in half verticaly if it helps.

- Left part put appointements and tasks (I dont have many appointments it's just a remind.
My appointment are on my Ical calendar synchronized with my iphone.
- Right part, put summerized notes about what happened or new information.
- In front of each task put a red dash if it is unfinished and follow.
- If something is due or planed at a certain date just put it on the corresponding future page.
- Use as a AF1 list date by date. (Mark Forster rules)
- If you dont have enought place for tasks goto where there is some

Well nothing to say it works.
Its so simple.
Its easy to carry anywhere
If an information is un usefull just cross it.
