Jacqueline:

It rather depends on how long your list is. A page in AF1 contains 34 tasks in my notebook. The total number of tasks in one's AF4 list at the moment of closing the open list could be anything. Initially one's closed list will contain every task on the whole list. So on average I would expect the closed list to take longer to finish than an individual page on AF1.

But on the other hand, simply because the list initially contains everything you currently have to do the time it takes to clear it is more meaningful than with AF1 pages.
