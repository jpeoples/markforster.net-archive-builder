Laurence,

I've only used it a few days, and I certainly haven't used all its capabilities. You can create multiple tiny digital clocks that count down (or up) and sit on your desktop on top of all the other applications. So, I suppose you could create one countdown timer that goes off at 7:25:00pm, another for 7:27:30pm, another for 7:29:00pmj, another for 7:29:45pm, and another for 7:30:00pm and enable a (different?) sound for each of them.

The thing is, there is a digital timer staring out from your computer the whole time.
