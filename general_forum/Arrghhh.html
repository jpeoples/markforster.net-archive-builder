Tijl,

If I were in this situation, I would do the following:

1. Buy a new notebook (a nice one, if possible).
2. Enter a task to dismiss the pages in the old notebook.
3. Enter a task to review the dismissed tasks in the old notebook.
4. Relax and trust that any important or urgent tasks will come to mind, and just enter them in the new notebook as they do.

And then, when you get back, you will probably be surprised how many of the old-notebook tasks are already done or reentered from your memory, so there won't be that many left to dismiss/review/reenter.

Enjoy your trip!
