PLEASE do not put comments on spam threads for the following reasons:

1) It encourages the spammers to spam again if they see they are getting a reaction.

2) If I'm not here to remove them, commenting on them won't get them removed any quicker.

3) Commenting on them brings them back to the top of the pile instead of letting them sink into well-deserved obscurity.

4) When I do delete the thread it is possible that your comment will be treated as spam and you may be liable to find yourself blocked in the future as a spammer.
