I have a method for keeping track of the thread posts (using OneNote but I think you could do the same with Word & PrintScreen)

I have a blank page in OneNote and use the Screen Clip utility to extract a screenprint of the thread list once I have read all of the threads. The next time I access the forum I can then easily see the last few threads that I had read and only need to look at posts above that point. Once I have read up to date again, I go back to my OneNote page, hit the undo button to remove the last screenclip, and then reclip the "new" position. It takes a matter of seconds to do and makes it a LOT easier ......
