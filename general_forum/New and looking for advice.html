Hi, Alison

Welcome to my site. I think you will find everyone very helpful here.

My recommendation is that you should keep one list for each location. Since you work from home, that would mean one list for you. There's no real need to colour-code and it's easiest just to lump everything in together.

Normal sized projects can usually be managed on the list, as long as you remember to include plenty of management tasks.

The sort of tasks I am talking about here begin with these sorts of verbs:

Think about…

Investigate…

Discuss… with …

Plan…

Review…

List…

You can probably think of more for yourself.

As for someday stuff, it's best to use the list only for projects which are active at the moment (or which you are thinking of making active). On the other hand when an idea comes to you, it's often a good idea to put it on the list as a query "Sail round the world next year?" if the answer is "no", then delete it and forget it. If the answer is "yes", start planning!
