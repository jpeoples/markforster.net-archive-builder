All this productivity is all well and good but so much of it is cranking widgets on the treadmill of familiarity and repetition in the comfort zone. SO as a reminder I have replaced my super-focus list in the top right of my AF page with a box - The Magic Box - where I pause to consider the magic in my life today, or this week, or where I want there to be magic. It acts as a source of "magical" activities that can be put on the AF list, or a reminder to take time to smell the roses, or to just pause and reconsider my trajectory.
