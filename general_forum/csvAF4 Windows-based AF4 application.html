Andreas:

Okay. Since you are interested (which makes me happy), let me announce the new version. The current one is indeed the 4th version; I have uploaded for possible silent users.

http://dl.dropbox.com/u/94280/csvAF4.zip

Now you will see just three files generated:
- List (including Closed and Open lists)
- Done (configurable)
- Maybe

I still think it is beneficial in my system to have separated "Done" and "Maybe" lists from currently-working "List" file. These lists are different in nature, and the first two can become large gradually but are deletable at any time if users don't need. On the other hand, the working file can stay reasonably small.

Your 0.9 basically worked fine. Like you, I didn't have enough time to use it so that I am going to full-review it this weekend again.

> My normal rule is that anything which I can't or don't want to do
> within two days goes into some form of reminder system.
> The reminder is not to do the task, but to put it on my AF list.
