You don't have to loop over and over if you're worried about your open list. That worry gives you permission to say no to each task, and then you can look at your open list.

Once you have gone over your open list, you can stop worrying because you now know whether you have important stuff there.

Back in the closed list, you again have permission to say no to stuff if you know something important is in your open list. Your only obligation is to scan the list, do one thing, scan again, do nothing, and move on.
