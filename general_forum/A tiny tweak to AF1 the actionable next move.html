Some of my entries on my AF1 list are not immediately actionable because they are too vague or generalised. In such cases I put a dash after the heading and think of the first actionable step. For example:

"weekend away?"

becomes

"weekend away? - check website X for holiday deals this weekend"

which can become

"weekend away? - check website X for holiday deals this weekend - book south coast stay on website Y"

The process helps me get clear on the next actionable step.
