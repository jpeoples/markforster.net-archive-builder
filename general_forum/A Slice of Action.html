A quick note for the newbies and anyone interested in getting started on anything, but just can't seem to get going.

I have a current project that I've really been struggling with and now a deadline has been imposed by a third party. Even with a deadline, I sometimes find it difficult to get under way and make progress.

So I've returned to the Salami Slice solution and it's still working for me (note to self: why don't I use it more often? Another note to self: don't get too distracted by introspection).

I set 5 minutes this morning on my kitchen timer on a shelf in my home office. Each time it beeps, I add another five minutes and set it running again, and add five minutes every time.

So I've now done 5 + 10 + 15 . . . . + 40 + 45 minutes = 225 minutes excluding a short breakfast break.

Now to go for a walk on this lovely Spring day here in England (yes, we do have them sometimes) before returning to start again at 5 minutes, amazing how one slides into the groove and progress is made.
