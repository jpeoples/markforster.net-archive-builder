Hi Jens,

I have a lot of urgent work, but I would suggest not doing so and following Mark's instructions. I see it a lot like the current inititiative in DIT -- do some of your important, backlog work first, then deal with urgent stuff later. I know this sounds silly, but I am finding it to work well. If there is something truly urgent and important, you will find yourself gravitating to that very quickly anyway.

Another thing I do is to put tasks that absolutely have to be done today in another list in Outlook and/or as an entry on my calendar. Depending on the importance of the urgent matter, I may work on that straight off before my AF list. But most times....at least for me...I am finding that I can work AF4 according to Mark's instructions. We will see how this works over the long period, but for now, it is working well for me.
