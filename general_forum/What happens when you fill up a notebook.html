Donna,

You have a choice between using both the old and new notebooks until everything is finished in the old one (which is what I think I will probably do), or alternatively transferring all open items to the new notebook. If you do that, be careful to maintain the page structure. So, if there are two items on the first open page, transfer both items to the new notebook and draw a line right across the page to signify that the two items represent one page. If you try to consolidate all the items into a new page structure you will mess up the system.
