Warning: I haven't yet been entirely successful with this idea. I'm presenting it for the crowd to consider, suggest improvements, or explain why it shouldn't work.

The idea is to examine tasks you feel you should do but aren't. Maybe it's about to be dismissed. Seems to me there are three cases.
1. It's part of something bigger but this part isn't ready yet. Solution: Scale it Up to the bigger task and start figuring how to work on that.
2. It's too big and you aren't grabbed because of its size. Solution: Scale it Down. Pick a part of the task that you are ready to do.
3. You really don't need this. Throw it Out.
