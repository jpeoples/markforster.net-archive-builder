I also used Autofocus to complete my thesis. I filled my list with tasks like "research for chapter 4", "proofread section 2.2", "write section 3.1" and so on. You could try the same for the information you need to learn for your exams. Either break it down to small things, or have a list with "study for exam A", "study for exam B". I found breaking it down made it easier for me to overcome procrastination.
