DKD:

"Someday items" is a GTD concept not an Autofocus one, and it's always a bit dangerous importing the concepts of one system into another.

Basically my advice is that you shouldn't try to dredge up every possible thing that you could, should or might do.

Instead start from scratch and let your list grow organically. By that I mean you add things as you think of them or as they come up. That way the list remains live and relevant.
