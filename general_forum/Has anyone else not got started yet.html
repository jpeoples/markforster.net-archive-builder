Reply by Christine B

2009-01-8 20:55
Writing the items on the list definately jogs the memory. I have some days with very restrictive time and the need to be reactive but have found that since starting AF this week my reactions have been different. After one phone call I just added the action points to my list, whereas I would previously have either tried to do the items immediately at the expense of what I was already doing, or would have worried about when I would find the time. On another phone call I requested that the person call me back at a more convenient time, whereas I would previously have allowed the interruption.

It really is the application of the system that somehow changes the focus, but don't panic about the time needed - just write what you can and try the process as recommended. Practicing on household chores over the weekend may help to get some experience as to how the system works .......
