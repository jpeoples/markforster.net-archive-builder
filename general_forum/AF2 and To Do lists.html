I'm asking this in a spirit of genuine interest, having been a keen user of AF since the start and now planning to try out AF2. However in AF2 the closed list concept virtually disappears, which led me to wonder about the journey you have made since DIT. On the front page of this website you still say about To Do lists: "A to do list is the finest known way of ensuring that you never get to the end of your work. The proof?
