DWM already has the second column although it isn't visually distinguished. Try it though: When you reenter a task on day W, put that task in column 2 (or just mark it as virtually in column 2). Then read http://www.markforster.net/blog/2011/2/9/handling-various-tasks-in-superfocus.html and see what makes sense.

Maybe reenter Unfinished on tomorrow instead of next week to get a similar forcefulness?
