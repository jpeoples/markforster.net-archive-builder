I read your article. It's a great talk on the Closed List. My current practice involves two closed lists and they are motivation in the way you describe. Adapting my Sooner or Later process, the Closed lists are my guarantee things will be tackled. The way it works in part is I add a bunch of stuff to Later, then draw a line. Every thing above the line is the closed list. Below the line is an open list. Whenever I select items from Later to act on, I always start by picking one or more Closed section items. So over time the closed list becomes empty. And then I close off the open part of this list and start a new open section.

This much is judt like AF4, but I'm not looping the Closed list. And the role is to feed into the Sooner list which has a similar open/closed structure.
