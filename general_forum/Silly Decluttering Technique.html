FVP.

1. Grab the first bit of clutter you see.
2. Throw it out.
3. No? Okay put it away.
4. No room? Take the first thing in that spot that matters less, and “dot it.”
5. Go to step 2 with your latest item.
6. If you clear all dots, go back to 1.
