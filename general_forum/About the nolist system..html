Little post just to tell you what happened with me with the no list system I have tried since a week now. Well, the result is a total wash out.

As with the list I was doing more or less the most important things quiet fastly, the no list sytem led me directly crash to the wall making me doing not the most important things but what I felt urging more or less and pleased me. I have increased my procrastination by a 100% spending a lot of time in the clouds instead of doing real oprationnal things.

Check list were OK, habits OK too but real difficulties about acting on the flow...

Here is what happened :

Negative :
- No feeling about things,
- No intuition,
- Un able to know what is next and necessary doing now about projects and goals
- Doing wrong things instead of doing the right thing at the right moment,
- Feeling of confusion and lost about what to do and when,
- Lose of self confident in the system and in me,
- Little list of stuff done but after daily control not the important things most of the time
- I Forgot 2 important dead lines and was unable doing what I ought to do...
- Lack of implication in task and projects
- Lack of visualisation about the global system all what stuck in the molasse of my mind,
- Un able to choose the right things to do when a stone get into the system ie 5 tasks written, at
the same time incoming huge and important interuption. My brain was just un able to choose what to do next...

Positive
- Easy controling at the end of the day.
- Feeling of doing things right (but is was a mirage)
- No list, so no control
- Fast to control my project list and goals (but no global view)

So I decided to stop the experiment yesterday morning getting back to a list and planning stuff as I explained before. Since the results has increased by a 100% and I feel much better !

My system has evoluated by basics as I explained before in another post using GTD, MF and Mac cormack principles.

So the system OF NO LIST is not convenient for every body. For me it is the worst system I ever tried. But now I know what to avoid and how. It was necessary to give it a try.

Thoughts ?

Hello ! OK - As promised I give you the basic and let's see after.

***PAPER***

I. CONTROL :
you will need 3 spiral paper A4 note book . keep 2 pages at the beginning for summary. Number pages.

*One in for all incoming information*. Just collect all what is incoming. I mean information not tasks. This is crucial. IE I receive information about building and client's needs. They goes in there. Date each day. Separates all information regarding to each people. IE I receive a call about Henri who speaks about building, research, negociation I write HENRY x, y, z one under the other and then I cross a line. Read it regularly. cross what you dont need anymore. You may add ideas, emails, notes about something the important is crossing after to see it and dating to know when.

*One is for task* Collect all tasks. Just collect AF way. One under the other. A waiting for write !
a task write ! dont use color or anything. Just write one under the other. Make ⅔ pages for crucial list mine are BUSINESS/OPPORTUNITIES- OBLIGATION- PERSONNAL STUFF. These list are extracted from the main list and listed. They are my crucial guidline per day. I treat them in a AF way but with no dissmissal as I cross everything little by little during my daily and weekly review. The list is just a warehouse for controling, thinking and doing on a daily basis. If something stand at me I do it.

*One is your historical note book" it is your journal. Note what you think, what happen and DATE ! Mine looks like this sunday 28th it happened this week this and this, I thougy that and that. Regarding to the situation I would do this and this. Use MF questioning Why and how. If you need during the day to think about something use it. Make a clear separation betweek items. Makes titles.

***COMPUTER***

*Create a folders ACTIVE* > It is your project list ie ORGANISATION > BUILDING X, Client Y

*Create a folders REFERENCES* > It is your GTD references (ie building plans, lease, acount..-
You will note in each file all historical stuff coming from your papers note book during your weekly review.

II. PERSPECTIVE :
This is a crucial part. Here your make a vision of What happened, were you are and where you are going to.

In developpement the basic idea is using heuristic schemes to vision short and long term goals/
Also use files for detailling goals like this GOAL X - I vision THIS- The steps are X, Y, Z
> Report steps in agenda for control > Report steps in your task list.

DO you weekly review on thursday. report everywhere so you can act on wednesday about urgencies.


What is the effect with me.

Collecting incoming information is fantastic. I can take my note book with me and find any information about anything in front of my clients. No needs other list exept building list and clients list (they are on excel because of calc and yields.)

Collecting the general list free me. I dont control anything. I feel quiet. I know every tasks is in there. When I am outside I collect on a piece of paper I have all the time with me (with a pen) I jot all information, tasks, ideas then they will be reported on my papers notebook and agenda if necessary.

Journaling is great I see the progress. I can read all at a glance. I highline important things everywhere when I am reluctant or if I need to focus on a special and important thing.

I never forget any thing. The core system is my daily and weekly review. I am building process (habits) to do things. Ie my habit is planning for now One project per day. Nothing else. I jot on my agenda DO projet X on monday.

I take time blocked for thinking about complicated project or stratégies.

As you see the basic idea is to make a clear separation between control and perspective. To keep intuition. To avoid computer except for what is absolutly necessary. To cross little by little unusefull information during the daily review (I use check list) To keep focus on goals.

There is nothing complicated. I only use specific list I i need it. The system cost nothing. It is always operational. When I am with a client I have my collect book. When I am at my office I use all books and computer.

In fact I did date realizing that I was fed up of complicated systems. Questions and organizational systems. During one meditation I realized that my brain perfectly know what to do and that I needed to trust my gut and intuition. I spent a little time doing AF MF list then Jane westmann which was similary except in the treatement. Then I realized that I needed a guidline I made a list of goals, after I planed my day strictely (Mac cormack) then I used covey and Eisenhowen rules (extracting core items in A, B, C, D) and now I am back to essential. I realized sudently that I had to many information, to many things to do and that my real problem was that I am un able to settle time. I mean to know at a glance when happened something (was it in 93 ? in 2003 ?) it is really complicated for me. So dating everything settle time. I even date time. Clients are sometime astonished when I say we spoke about thins and this on last tuesday at 4:25PM . I dont have a good memory about this. I only have a good note book. Reading it regularly helps me to decide what is the best for me.

Hope that's help.
