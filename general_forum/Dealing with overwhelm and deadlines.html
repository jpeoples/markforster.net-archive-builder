Recently I've found myself resisting using AF.It's just that theres so much entered that the thought of reviewing the whole notebook to make sure I wasnt missing deadlines felt like too much work.
I should know better-I should trust the system!
But that feeling of having to do a daily review was sort of damper on my "intuition".

So what I've now done is entered items with deadlines on the appropriate line,but UPSIDE DOWN and in CAPITALS.
So if I want to see just the deadlines I need to work toward,I just turn the notebook upside down and these items are obvious.I then work on these in usual manner.

Appeals to my creative side and doesnt seem to break the system or overcomplicate it.
Just felt like sharing this.

Cheers
