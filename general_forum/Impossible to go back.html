A strange thing is happening to me.

A week ago I decided to go back to AF1, along with my REVERSE tweaks. Every page a closed list, dismiss when no item stands out, et cetera et cetera.

But... It simply did not happen. Before I realized it, I had drawn the AF2 dismissal line every morning. Later I "just had to look for the last page again". And so on. The lists in my notebook didn't want to be closed again.

Maybe it's just that I have to overcome my "last page weakness" (or "glueness"). I'll TRAIN myself to go back through the pages more decisively, by REALLY pointing and looking at every item on the list one after the other, waiting for the "standing out".
