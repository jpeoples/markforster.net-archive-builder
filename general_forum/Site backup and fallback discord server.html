Hello everyone,

since there have been concerns regarding Mark's whereabouts and well-being and the future of this site, I have created a backup copy of the whole site and uploaded it to archive.org.

It can be found here: http://archive.org/details/markforster.squarespace.com

It's a zip archive that's 447 mb compressed and 2.2 gb uncompressed. After unpacking it, you can open any of the html files (index.html is the start page) and browse the site locally on your computer. All forum posts and articles are preserved.

The current state of the site backup is from March 7th, 2025. I will occasionally update the backup to include newer forum posts.

Also, as Nico suggested in the thread "Mark update? Site is slow?", I also created a Discord server that could serve as a fallback to meet up if this site ever goes offline. I'm not convinced Discord is a good match for the kind of discussions that take place here, but it's something.

Here is the invite link to the Discord server: https://discord.gg/4vm8CtHY

Hopefully both measures will not be needed for a long time, and if Mark or anyone else objects, I will take them both down again.

Regards,
Andreas
