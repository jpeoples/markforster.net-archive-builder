Systems Used:

- Day list. About 10 LAOs, then about 15 tasks or projects are urgent or are bothering me. Do not erase from other lists; worrisome projects have several entries.

- Morning LAO block. Doing several LAOs in the morning works well, but not so many that I neglect the other projects. Do in order that makes sense or feels right at the moment.

- Randomizer. Use a large enough die to get me off the day list 1/4 of the time. Very rough count, and continue using same die as day list shrinks). Start on day list, then day lists for last week (keeps projects moving, often has tasks with "next week" deadlines), then to the bookmark in main list. Count "done" lines and slide as per Mark's system. Tweak: Near end of day, slid to task needed for meeting.

- Common Sense. Had to review the outstanding accounts to make report for meeting, and randomizer refused to choose it.
