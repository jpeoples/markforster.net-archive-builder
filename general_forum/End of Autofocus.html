Jupiter

No time management system will allow you to do the work of 3 people. You need to think of different approach, eg hire some resource, push back hard on any new tasks arriving - ask why its needed endlessly and if none of that works, leave.

Having said that I am sypathetic to your view on AF. I found it difficult to get an overview of where my projects (and their related tasks) were when they were spread out over many pages. The is problem is increased if you use an electronic solution where you show the data in many ways - I've found that I stopped trusing these electronic systems.
