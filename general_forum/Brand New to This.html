It seems like you've got it. I wouldn't focus on open/closed or on what day you are working from. Just cycle through all the pages. Right now, most are empty, but over the next month most will have entries added to them.

Be sure to have TODAY, REENTRY, and NEW tabs for Today, One Week Ahead, and One Month ahead. Some people have trouble remembering where to put stuff or just finding the right page.

If you remove old pages, you won't need a Today tab.

But if you don't, it represents your start point. You don't get to look at pages earlier than Today. The tab just marks that for you so when you get to the end, you know where to go.
