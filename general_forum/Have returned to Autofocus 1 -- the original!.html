Hi Claudia,

As you know, I think that AF4 is a great system. But I went back and reread the instructions for AF1 and realized that I really missed the simple one page blocks of AF1. Plus, believe it or not, I find I can get to the end of the list more quickly in AF1! With AF4, one must keep cycling through the closed list before one can move into the open list. I would find myself doing a lot of work on the closed list, which is good, but I would tend to stay there as things kept getting added to the end of the open list. With AF1, it seems easier to navigate through each closed page and before you know it, you are at the last page.

Hope that is clear and makes some sense....
