I work with AF since a week and it works quite well wih me
I found a few tips that i wish to share with you, may be you found others and it could be a nice list.

1. i put all my items one by one with always a project name (usually one or two words)
2. i highline in orange all my project
3. When i wait for something or a message i use this shortcut ③ and color it in GREEN, dito if it's a message to call me back with M$
4. All due date is mentionned at the end of the item and the date is colored it in pink
5. All my items wich have to be done today has a * (star) in front of them
6. My notebook is diveded in two verso has my professional tasks, recto has my personnal tasks
7. i write my notes like an item one after the others and treat them dido
8. i put every thinking on my notebook watever they may be
9. i always add all my substacks when i write the task and go as far as i can
10. I use little and shortly mind map in front of the items when things become complicated or if i hesitate or think about solutions.

The result is
i have a better focus on what i do
i work faster
i easely can find my items
