Hi Walter,

I'm now on DWM.xls v1.1.2 with two small tweaks ...
(1) I have an additional column I for "Links", so I had to do manual import from DWM_old.xls. I do Ctrl-Shift-Z to disable events then copy necessary open/expired tasks. Not much prob.
(2) I commented out the "JumpToActiveTask" in Ctrl-Shift-C, so it's consistent with Ctrl-Shift-E & R acting on current task not active task.
... works great.

I also like to report a small bug in v1.1.2 and v1.1.1 ...
* If there is a pending dismissal, and you do Ctrl-Shift-R, after dismissal the wrong task is Re-Entered (though the right tasks are dismissed, and right task is closed).
* My current work-around is to do a Ctrl-Shift-S before any work in the morning, so dismissals (if any) would be done correctly.
