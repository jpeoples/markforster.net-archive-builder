It makes quiet a long time that I work with AF and SF.

I guess It is time to make a real point about it and what happened in my life or yours because of the system.

- First the real improvement happened when I decided to do it on paper, ONLY ! and decided to make it simple. I simply took a paper spiral note book and wrote everything one after the other each new day starting by the date.

When I mean everything I might say "actionable" i.e. tasks, Thought and ideas and notes goes to another notebook. All others informations goes to my diary, my paper folders, others note books....

Then I noticed 2 kind of situation.
The day was quiet I worked in AF way.
The day was hot I worked in SF way

Then I noticed that I the end of the day there was a kind of daily review which purpose was to eliminate the maximum of information from the list.

Still I worked like this I can tell you that I feel very comfortable.
The system goes fast, intuitively with a great elimination of the items.

I dont do any daily list, I don't take time to make others list.
I work day by day.
If something still stay in the previous day it is that is is dismissed or it doesn't worth to satay on the list.

Except this, I only work on some projects when I have time too.
I have an unfinished project list and future project list.

I am very happy which my list. The system is great.
