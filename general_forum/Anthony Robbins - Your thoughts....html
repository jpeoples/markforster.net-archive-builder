BTW, in case anyone is interested in persuing this whole approach further ...

Tony Robbins has repackaged some of the most easily accessible ideas from NLP (Neuro Linguistic Programming) with some of the more traditional time management and basic success ideas such as goal setting and making and following a To Do list.
