I recently purchased a book entitled "The Art of Rest" by Claudia Hammond.
It is based on a survey of 18,000 people. A chapter is devoted to the top ten answers to what activities they find most restful. The book is well-written, and cites research for support.

Counting down:

10 Mindfulness
9 watching TV
8 daydreaming
7 a nice hot bath
6 a good walk
5 doing nothing in particular
4 listening to music
3 I want to be alone

1 Reading
