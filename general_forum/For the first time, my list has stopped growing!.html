I have tried all DIT, AF1 - 4, and it would appear that now with DWM Mark has successfully dealt with the issue of the ever-growing list. I'm a few days into the second month and am keeping note of the total number of tasks in the status bar of my ListPro software.

So well done, Mark, you are step by step dealing with the issues that have cropped up since AF1.
