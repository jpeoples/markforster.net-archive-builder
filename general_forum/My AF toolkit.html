I just thought I'd share with everyone what I use for my AF system, maybe others could comment on whether it sounds right or not...?

I have an A4 spiral-bound notebook for my master AF list, a pen, a ruler (for crossing off!) and my diary - this is used for appointments which I don't put onto my AF list. These items are all put into an A4 "button folder" which is carried around with me!

I also have an A5 notebook plus pen in my car, in an A5 "button folder" for any ideas whilst I'm on the move.

Does that sound like I'm carrying the right tools with me...?

I would be interested to hear any feedback on this, whether I'm doing it right by entering appointments in my diary rather than my AF list.
