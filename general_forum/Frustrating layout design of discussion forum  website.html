I'm loathe to make a negative comment about a site which has given me so much benefit at no cost whatsoever ...

However, I was wondering if I'm the only person who finds the website a bit frustrating to use?

On the one hand, it's clear and simple to get around with no unnecessary gimmicks or confusing graphics - which is great. But on the other hand, every time I go onto the site (the Discussion Forum section or otherwise), I'm faced with a column of text on the left and a column of text on the right, and then what appears to be a column in the middle which is empty. To get to any of the 'content', I have to scroll down a page or two before I get to the content in the left hand column.

If I click on the different menu 'sections' along the top, I'm faced with the exact same page each time ... unless I scroll down and look at the bottom of the left hand column. When I revisited the site for the first time in a while, I thought it was malfunctioning at first; and if I was a new visitor, I probably wouldn't have bothered to look further (akin to judging a book by its cover).

Of course, this isn't the end of the world, but I haven't experienced this frustration on any other website (and I've visited a fair number in my time), and it seems in such contrast with the fantastic content within the website itself.

For the record, I use a regular sized laptop (Dell Latitude D630 if you really wanted to know) and my screen settings are all normal. I've faced the same problem with the site for a good while and I've not had any similar difficulties on any other website.

Once again - apologies if this has already been discussed previously (I did carry out a brief search in the discussion forum just in case).
