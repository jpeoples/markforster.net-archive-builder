Jason:

First of all make sure you are doing it properly. It's not just a matter of "looking down the list". After you have read quickly through the page, you should start moving down the list using a pointer (your pen will do) to stop momentarily at each item until one stands out. Then do as much work as you feel like on that item, cross it out and re-enter it if necessary.

If that's not happening for you, then it would certainly help to break the jobs down further. It will probably take a little while to get your mind accustomed to the fact that it doesn't "have" to do anything.

Daily tasks should be entered on the list like any other task. in fact you will probably find that your response to email is greatly speeded up and you clear it more than once a day.
