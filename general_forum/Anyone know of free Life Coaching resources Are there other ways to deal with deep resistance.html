Dear Jon

New life-coaches are often willing to take on free clients in order to get practice. If you do a web search for coaching schools in your part of the world, you might be able to make contact with their students or recent graduates.

On the subject of getting a good time management system going, I would advise you to concentrate on making the Do It Tomorrow system work. An intellectual understanding is no use - you need to practise it.

If you haven't started with it yet, there is a quick start guide right at the front of the book which will get you going very quickly indeed. If you have already started then go to the end of the book and fill in the check-list (page 202) to see what you are not doing right. Direct your attention to the items you haven't ticked.
