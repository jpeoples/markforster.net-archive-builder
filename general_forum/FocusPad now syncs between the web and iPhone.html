Hi all.

As some of you know, I am the author of FocusPad, an iPhone app that can be used instead of paper to implement all versions of AF.

Just thought I would let you know that I have released an update to FocusPad that syncs with the web. This was the #1 feature request, so I am sure this will make many of you happy.

The web version of FocusPad is at http://focuspad.ws. You can use it with or without the iPhone app. Just use your Google account to log in (it uses Google App Engine).

Note that the web version is still under development, and it is bound to get slicker and faster over the coming months.

(The next update to FocusPad will have multiple pads, ticklers, and search. I hope to have that ready in 4-6 weeks.)
