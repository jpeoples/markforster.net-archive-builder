I would be most obliged if some of the readers of this forum would share a couple statistics from their AutoFocus (version 1 or 2) lists. They are:

1. How many open pages do you have?

2. What time frame do you intend your list to be aimed at?

I think the first question is pretty straight-forward. Feel free to add additional detail if you want, like what your typical number of open pages is and how many lines you have per page. But just a simple number is fine.

For the second I just mean your intuitive sense of the time frame during which you hope to process most, or even all, of the items on your list. Some people like to have a to-do list that is just focused on the next few days. Others might have one aimed at a period of months.

My answers:

List 1, professional: 4 open pages, 33 items/page; about two weeks.

List 2, personal: 12 open pages, 33 items/page; about two months.

Thanks!
