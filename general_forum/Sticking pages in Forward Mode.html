This was mentioned in several posts in the last time: One enters Forward Mode, but arrives soon at always the same page where nothing stands out... so one goes back to Reverse Mode... and the pages in the middle of the list remain untouched for a long time.

I suggest a simple addition to the rule: If you get to a certain page in Forward Mode where nothing stands out FOR THE SECOND TIME IN A ROW, don't switch back to Reverse Mode, but dismiss the page instead and go on to the next page (as in classical AF1).

Works great!
