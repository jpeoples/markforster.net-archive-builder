Apologies if this thought has been aired already: I would not be surprised, though I can't immediately see it anywhere.

AF4 isn't the only system to which recurring and unfinished baskets can be added. It can be added also to DWM, which becomes much simplified.

One has a list of dated items. Anything that's been on there for more than a month gets discarded. One chooses from the list something (or perhaps three things) to work on. After at least some small advance is made, a task is completed, in which case it can be ticked off altoghether; or it is placed in the recurring or unfinished baskets for further attention later. The unfinished basket replaces entirely the need for the notion of weekly dismissal.

I'm finding this dramatically effective.
