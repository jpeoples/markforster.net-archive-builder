I ran into that bug when you enter a new task with the same name. I put a space after the word, then it works. I use the "step" swipe on the left for many repeating items because it is fast. The right swipe give you two options, select or "more" which has "complete task" as well as other options and is easier to use on my phone. If you accidentally complete a task you want to bring back, swipe from the right then duplicate but add the space.

What I like about Dado is it shows how FVP works. Your selected list is arranged with newest task at the top and the first selected task at the bottom. And if you add something new to the selected list it comes up on top. You can also make a task inactive and when it goes back on the list it is in the same order as it started. I keep my selected list to 3 to 5 items.

I have tried to figure out how to get other electronic apps to do this sorting. Larger type would be nice too.
