Dear Juman

The answer is that you carry your list forward to the following day. What this in practice means is that instead of doing a batch of one day's email you do a batch of two days, instead of one day's paper you do two days'. Any tasks undone in your Task Diary are carried forward to the next day too.

This doesn't matter because you are aiming to complete one day's incoming work per day *on average*. However very importantly this means that you must catch up after a few days. If you find yourself constantly behind, you need to go through the diagnostic procedure: have I got too much work? am I working efficiently? am I leaving enough time?
