I currently use the “Do It Tomorrow” system. It’s very effective for me except for what Autofocus takes into account -- can’t finish today / don’t want to finish today.

Sometimes using DIT means I miss deadlines because working one project to completion means overshooting the deadline for another project. (Too much on my plate?)

What I really like about DIT – the “Will Do Today” list vs. “everything else” on the “Do It Tomorrow” list.

Maybe I’ll end up with a hybrid of DIT/Autofocus, but initially I will try AF to compare the two.
